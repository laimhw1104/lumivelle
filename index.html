Frequently Asked Questions

常見問題

What is included in the your packages? 標準套餐包括什麼服務？

All of our packages include Fujifilm Instax (Mini) prints, a customized guestbook, station decoration, signage pen, and 2 professional staff members. For rates and charges, we will send you a Rate Card upon submitting a booking enquiry.

我們的套餐包括無限量 Fujifilm Instax Mini 相紙、客製化留言相簿、留言區佈置、簽名筆，以及兩位專業工作人員服務。請經 WhatsApp 傳送您的婚禮資料以獲取報價詳情。

How many photos are included? 我們可以拍攝多少張相片？

We provide a generous, fixed amount of films based on your estimated guest count and package of your selection to complete your album, while ensuring every guest has a chance to take home one of their favourite prints.

我們會根據您所選擇的套餐及預計賓客人數提供充足且固定數量的相紙，確保相簿能夠完整，以及每位賓客都能帶走一張最喜歡的相片。

Is the film supply unlimited? 相紙供應是無限量的嗎？

To ensure a neat and organized guestbook and to manage on-site logistics, we provide a pre-agreed quantity of films that effectively covers your guest list.

為確保留言相簿質素並有效管理現場運作，我們會提供事先約定好的相紙數量，足以覆蓋您的賓客名單。

Do we need to provide a table or power? 我們需要提供桌子或電源嗎？

We usually request one long table from the venue. Since Instax cameras are battery-operated, we generally do not require a power outlet.

我們通常需要場地提供一張長桌。由於即影即有相機使用電池運作，我們一般不需要電源。

Can we customize the guestbook? 我們可以自訂留言相簿嗎？

Yes! For your album, you may select your own cover photo, the colour and text fonts of our selection. For design reference, please check out our portfolio or send us an enquiry.

可以！留言相簿封面的文字、相片均可由您自行選擇，另外相簿亦有幾種顏色可供選擇，詳情可前往我們的社交平台參考或聯絡我們。

What does the station decoration include? 留言區佈置包括什麼？

Every wedding is special, so we ensure that the setup is tailored to you! Items may include (but not limited to) table linens, silk floral arrangements, decorative photo frames, and elegant signage that matches your wedding theme.

我們認為每個婚禮都是特別的，所以我們會專門訂製屬於您的佈置。我們提供(但不限於)檯布、絲花佈置、裝飾相框以及符合簡約粉色系主題的優雅指示牌。

How many staff will be there? 現場會有多少位工作人員？

We always provide two staff members. One focuses on photography and camera technicals, while the other assists guests with sticking photos and writing messages.

我們固定提供兩位工作人員。一位專注於拍攝及相機運作，另一位則協助賓客黏貼相片及撰寫留言。

How early will you arrive to set up? 你們會提前多久到達現場佈置？

Our team arrives an hour before the service starts to ensure the station is perfectly decorated and ready for the first guest.

我們的團隊會於服務開始前一小時到達，確保佈置完美並準備好迎接第一位賓客。

What if the wedding is outdoor? 如果是戶外婚禮怎麼辦？

We love outdoor weddings! In case of rain, we just require a covered area or a backup plan to protect the guestbook and equipment.

我們非常歡迎戶外婚禮！只要有遮蓋處或遇雨時的備案，以保護留言冊及器材即可。

How far in advance should we book? 我們應該提前多久預約？

Popular wedding dates in Hong Kong fill up quickly. To ensure the highest quality of our service, please confirm your booking at least one month in advance before your Big Day.

香港熱門的婚禮日子通常很快爆滿，為了提供最佳的服務體驗，請於服務使用日前至少一個月作出預約。

How do we confirm the booking? 如何確認預約？

After checking availability via WhatsApp, a 50% deposit is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is settled within 7 days after the wedding.

經 WhatsApp 確認檔期後，需支付 50% 定金以保留日子。尾款請於完成婚禮後七天之內結清。
